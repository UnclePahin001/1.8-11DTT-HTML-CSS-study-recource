Science | 1.2 Pysics of an Application

NCEA Level One Science

In this achievement standard, you will be understanding the physics of an application through modeling. You will be analyzing physics concepts/phenomena in the taiao (environment) which you will then evaluate and integrate evidence to do so. The level of achievement that you will reach is decided by the way you process information. The key words are describe, explain, and discuss the use of physics knowledge. Your teacher will give you written instructions of: possible research applications; the way to collect and process information; and the way to present your report.

Achievement

Demonstrate understanding of the physics application


Achievement with Merit

Demonstrate in-depth understanding of the physics of an application


Achievement with Excellence

Demonstrate comprehensive understanding of the physics of an application


Advice and tips

To make the absolute most of your exam and excel yourself,

Make sure you:
- follow the written instructions that will be given to you by your teacher
- research should include at least three resources. Gather your resources and make copies that you can show your teacher
- gather information from different types of resources including, but not limited to, books, magazines, people, internet
- use the assessment resource provided to you as a guideline
- do not use the same applications as provided in the assessment resource. Make sure the report is your own work
